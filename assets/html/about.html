About Tourest

Discover. Plan. Travel Better.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Tourest?
Tourest is a travel platform offering curated tours, destination ideas, and seamless booking for a better travel experience.
How do I book a tour?
Browse our Tours or Locations page, select a package you love, then proceed to Booking to submit your request. Our team will confirm shortly.
Can I change or cancel my booking?
Yes. Policies vary by tour. Contact support with your booking code and we’ll assist with the best available option.
Is my payment secure?
We use trusted payment gateways and do not store sensitive card data on our servers.
